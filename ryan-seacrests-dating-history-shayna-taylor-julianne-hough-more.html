Ryan Seacrest's Dating History: Shayna Taylor, Aubrey Paige, More

September 2024 · 3 minute read

As one of Hollywood’s hottest TV hosts, it’s no surprise Ryan Seacrest has dated a handful of lucky ladies throughout his iconic career. While he’s been in some serious relationships with women including Julianne Hough, Shayna Taylor and more, he has most recently been linked to model Aubrey Paige Petcosky.

Unfortunately, the Live With Kelly and Ryan star and the stunning food blogger — who first became a couple in 2013 — called it quits for the third time in June 2020. “Ryan and Shayna decided to end their romantic relationship amicably some time ago,” his representative told People at the time. “They remain good friends, each other’s biggest supporters and will always cherish their time together as a couple.”

News of Ryan and Shayna’s split was first speculated when the Daily Mail published photos of the radio personality on vacation in Mexico with an unrecognizable woman that month. Ryan was spotted holding hands with the mystery blonde as they enjoyed some R&R during their romantic getaway.

Considering the on-and-off pair seemed happier than ever before their split, fans were shocked to find out Ryan and Shayna called it quits once again. The Emmy Award winner even gave a rare glimpse inside their relationship while chatting with People in May 2020. Because the ex-duo were in quarantine together amid the coronavirus pandemic, he dished how they were staying busy.

“[Shayna] and I are spending a lot of time in the kitchen,” he gushed at the time. “I feel like people all over the world are doing that. I’m doing things that I have not in a long time. Like the dishes. Loading the dishwasher. I have to tell you, I find it quite peaceful and relaxing. I never thought I’d want to go back to doing that again as not a chore.”

Not only that, but a source close to the former duo revealed Shayna was willing to move with her beau if he decided to move back home to Los Angeles. Although the two relocated to New York City together in March 2017, an insider told Closer that Ryan was thinking of heading back home as he “he loves L.A. and misses the lifestyle.”

Despite not having any plans set in stone, Shayna was willing to “follow” her man “wherever he sets up his home base,” the source noted. “He has nothing against New York, but he feels that he was healthier in L.A.”

While chatting on the topic of marriage, Ryan once dished why he never asked any of his former girlfriends for their hand in marriage. “I love the idea [of getting married],” he explained to cohost Kelly Ripa during an episode of the ABC talk show. “I know you’re going to ask me of marriage because my parents have been married for 47 years, and so I have this thing … I figure the longer I wait, the older I’ll be, the closer to death I am, so I can get it in, and not screw it up.”
