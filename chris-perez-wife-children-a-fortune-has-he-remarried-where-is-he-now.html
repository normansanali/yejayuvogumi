About Chris Perez

Christopher Gilbert Pérez is an American songwriter, guitarist and author. He was born to Gilbert Pérez and Carmen Medina on August 14, 1969 in San Antonio, Texas, United States. The Grammy Award winner was widely recognized as the lead guitarist of Tejano band Selena Y Los Dinos. He eventually dated and married the band’s lead singer, Selena.

Growing up for Chris was by no means smooth, due to his parents’ divorce when he was just four years old. Her mother remarried four years after the divorce. As a computer engineer, Chris had no interest in his father’s computer science profession. He grew up with an overflowing enthusiasm for music and a particular passion for the guitar. He adored Carlos Santana and learned most of his techniques and abilities.

He began his musical career with the group Tejano Selena Y Los Dinos. He was invited by the bassist of the band at the time, AB Quintanilla III. He left the band after Selena was murdered in 1995. Selena’s death completely ruined Chris psychologically and emotionally. It was so damaging to him that he started abusing drugs and consuming alcohol in excess.

Years passed, Chris got a little sober, he started a band he called the Chris Pérez band. He released his debut album, Resurrection which won him a Grammy Award for Best Latin Rock Album. The band soon broke up right after the release of their second album. Una Noche Mas . Since then, he tried to commit to a long-term project, but it didn’t work out. He moved to Kings of Kumbia which lasted from 2003-2006 and then to Kumbia All Starswhich lasted longer than the old one, from 2006-2011. His lack of progress has been blamed on his fans and the media for his full recovery from his unending grief for his late wife Selena. And the facts supporting that claim seemed to be true. In 2012, he wrote and published a book called To Selena, With Love.
