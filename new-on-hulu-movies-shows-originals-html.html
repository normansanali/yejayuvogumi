This Month’s Highlight

Self Reliance

After premiering at 2023’s SXSW festival, Jake Johnson’s (of New Girl) directorial debut is finally making its way to Hulu. Johnson plays a loner named Tommy Walcott who gets invited to play a dark web competition game worth $1 million dollars in winnings if he can survive it. Andy Samberg, Anna Kendrick, and Christopher Lloyd also star. (Streaming January 12.)
