CJ Perry Set To Appear On MTV Cribs Reboot

July 2024 · 2 minute read

CJ Perry has made a lot of money in the pro wrestling business, and she is very comfortable in her life. Fans will soon get a little peek in on the kind of crib she comes home to.

MTV has revealed that Cribs is set to make its return on Wednesday, November 15 at 9:30pm ET/PT. This revival will once again provide audiences with exclusive glimpses into the private lives of beloved celebrities.

In each episode, viewers will venture past security gates to uncover previously unseen content, ranging from extravagant themed rooms and well-stocked fridges to astonishing closets. Cribs offers an insider’s look into the homes of their favorite stars, granting them unparalleled access to the world of celebrities.

The reboot of MTV Cribs will feature a ton of big names. Those names include: Adrian Grenier, Adrienne Bailon-Houghton & Family, Alyssa Edwards, Amanda Seales, Anjali Bhimani, Atsuko Okatsuka, Bobby Berk, C.J. Perry, DaniLeigh, Dita Von Teese, E.G. Daily, Emelia Hartford, Gus Kenworthy, Janel Parrish, Jaren Jackson Jr., Jenna Jameson, Jesse Metcalfe, Jonathan Bennett, Kaila Novak, Margaret Cho, Martha Hunt, Mena Suvari, Nastia Liukin, Nick Viall, Nicole Sullivan, Nikki Garcia, Perez Hilton, Rumer Willis, Scotty Pippen Jr., Shaun T, Taye Diggs, Vanessa Williams, Vinny Guadagnino, and Vivica A. Fox.

Obviously, pro wrestling fans will be very interested to see what kind of life CJ Perry is living. It is unknown if Miro will make a cameo in that episode, but we can only imagine that he was around for the filming.
