The Meaning Behind The Song: “Feenin'” by Jodeci

The Introduction of “Feenin'”

“Feenin'” is a song by the American R&B group Jodeci, released in 1994. The song is about someone who is addicted to a lover and can’t get enough of their affection. Written by group member DeVante Swing, the track was an instant hit and peaked at number two on the Billboard Hot 100 and number one on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart.

The Lyrics of “Feenin'”

The lyrics of “Feenin'” express the deep feeling of wanting more from a lover. The first verse of the song goes:

“Feenin’ for your love
Sick, even in love
But girl, you’re the one
I want to be with
You make me feel so funny
You knock me off of my feet
And baby, for you, I’ll do
Anything and everything”

The song goes on to describe the addictive nature of the love the protagonist feels towards his lover. The chorus repeats the word “feenin'” multiple times, emphasizing the persistent desire he feels towards his lover.

The Meaning of “Feenin'”

The term “feenin'” is a slang word that originated in African American Vernacular English (AAVE), meaning to obsessively crave or want something. In this context, Jodeci is using the term to describe the protagonist’s addiction to his lover. The song captures the feeling of being in such an intense and passionate love that it becomes an obsession.

The Influence of “Feenin'” on R&B Music

“Feenin'” helped establish Jodeci as one of the leading R&B groups of the 90s. The song influenced the genre with its smooth harmonies and sexualized lyrics. Its success paved the way for other R&B groups such as Boyz II Men, Destiny’s Child, and TLC. The song was also covered by contemporary artists such as Drake and Trey Songz, proving its lasting impact on the R&B genre.

The Controversy of “Feenin'”.

Despite its success, “Feenin'” also faced some criticism for its explicit content. The song’s lyrics describe an obsession with a lover that some saw as unhealthy and bordering on stalker behavior. The music video also featured sexual imagery, which made some uncomfortable.

The Legacy of “Feenin'”

Despite the controversy surrounding its lyrics and music video, “Feenin'” remains a classic R&B song that captures the intense and sometimes obsessive nature of love. The song’s influence can still be heard in contemporary R&B music and its legacy as one of the defining songs of the 90s cannot be denied.
