Olivia Jade was thurst into the spotlight early after the college admissions scandal. Now that she’s 24, take a look back at her best photos.

Beauty influencer Olivia Jade Giannulli suddenly became famous for a VERY different reason in March 2019, when it was revealed that her parents, actress Lori Loughlin and fashion designer Mossimo Giannulli, allegedly bribed USC coaches with $500,000 to get her into the prestigious school. Her parents eventually pleaded guilty and both served short prison sentences. For Olivia, the fallout from the scandal meant she lost a sponsorship from Sephora and other top makeup brands.

Olivia did find other ways to capitalize on her newfound notoriety. Following the ordeal, the second-generation star found even larger social media fame and snagged a slot on ‘Dancing With The Stars’ in 2021. As of 2022, she is dating Jacob Elordi of ‘Euphoria’ fame.
