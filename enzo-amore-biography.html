Who is Enzo Amore?

Enzo Amore is an American professional wrestler and rapper. He performs on the independent circuit with the name, ‘no’. Amore is best known for his stint at WWE.

Enzo Amore: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Childhood, Ethnicity

Enzo Amore was born Eric Arndt in Hackensack, New Jersey on December 8, 1986. As of 2022, his age is 35. He spent his childhood days in Waldwick, New Jersey. He has not revealed anything about his parents. Similarly, there is no information about his siblings and other family members.

His ethnicity is German.

Education: School/College, University

Amore is a well-educated man. He joined Julia A. Traphagen School. After that, he transferred to Crescent School.

For high-school education, he chose Waldwick High School from where he graduated.

He played football in school and continued his football career by playing Division III (NCAA) for Salisbury University.

From Salisbury University, he graduated with a degree in journalism.

Enzo Amore: Professional Life, Career

In August of 2012, WWE signed him. He used the ring name, Eric Anthony. In November 2012, the NXT gave him the name, Enzo Amore. In May 2012, he made his debut which he lost against the veteran wrestler, Mason Ryan. Eventually, he ended up teaming with Colin Cassady. The duo named themselves ‘the most real guys in the room’.

He had to take complete bed rest for seven months after injuring his legs in November 2013. He returned in 2014 and the duo competed together in NXT Tag Championship followed by NXT Take Over Fatal Four-way.

In April 2016, the duo made their way to the main roster of WWE Raw. Together, the duo has defeated The Ascension, The Dudley Boyz, and many others.

He suffered another injury in May 2016 when he lay unconscious on the floor diagnosed with a deep concussion. His wrestling career ended in January 2018, followed the rape allegations.

Music Career

Enzo started his career with the stage name, Real1. His genre is Rap. He released his first song in May 2018 titled, ‘Phoenix’. He released his second single, ‘Bury Me a G’ on June 7.

On November 18, he released his first album, ‘Rosemary’s Baby Pt. 1: Happy Birthday.

How tall is Enzo Amore?

Amore stands at a height of around 5 feet 11 inches. He weighs around 91 kg. He has dark brown colored hair and blue eyes.

Rape Allegations

Amore was suspended on January 22, 2018, by WWE. WWE suspended him for violation of its zero-tolerance policy for matters involving sexual assault and harassment. The media company released a statement stating the wrestler would remain suspended until the matter was completely resolved.

On January 23, a woman named Philomena Sheahan accused him of raping her. She claimed in an interview with TMZ that the wrestler raped her in Phoenix, Arizona in a hotel room on October 19, 2017. Due to the allegations, he lost his contract with WWE and the Cruiserweight Championship.

He denied all the allegations made against him and the police also ceased their investigation due to insufficient evidence.
