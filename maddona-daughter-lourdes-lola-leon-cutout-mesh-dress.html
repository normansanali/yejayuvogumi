Madonaa's Daughter Lola Looks Rave-Ready at the Marc Jacobs Show

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Lourdes Leon is carrying out the bold fashion legacy of her famous mother, Madonna.

On Monday night, Lola stepped out for the Marc Jacobs Autumn 2024 show in New York City wearing a barely-there mesh dress. The ensemble—which seemed more fit for a rave than for sitting in the front row—featured a scoop neck and thin straps, as well as dozens of cutouts that exposed Leon’s torso and legs. Mesh inserts and a matching black underwear set were all that covered the model’s modesty, and the strings of her side-tie black briefs even stuck out of her dress.

Dimitrios Kambouris

To complement her risqué ‘fit, Lourdes wore large silver hoop earrings and a diamond tennis necklace. In a very Madonna move, she also added a silver pendant necklace bearing a small cross. In one hand, she carried a black leather handbag with silver hardware, while on the other, she wore an elegant black leather watch. She added some sparkle to her look with heeled mules that boasted crystal-embellished straps.

For glam, Leon wore her signature long black hair in effortless waves, and showed off a ‘90s-inspired French manicure. Her brown eyes were tightly lined with black eyeliner, bringing the edginess of her outfit full-circle. Sculpted brows and a glossy nude lip completed her makeup look.

Like her Material Girl mother, Lola knows how to pull off a daring outfit. Earlier this year, she stepped out in a silky leopard-print minidress and paired the look with fuzzy black peep-toe heels. Before that, she made her risky fashion preferences clear while attending the Yves Saint Laurent menswear show in Paris, where she wore an entirely see-through black catsuit that featured a lacy floral pattern and thick black bands that censored her waist and neckline.
