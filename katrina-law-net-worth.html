Katrina Law Wiki Biography

Katrina Law is an actress, born on 30th September 1985 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA. She is probably best known for her roles in TV series “Spartacus: Blood and Sand”, “Spartacus: Vengeance” and “Arrow”.

Have you ever wondered how rich Katrina Law is? According to sources, it has been estimated that Katrina Law’s overall net worth is $2 million, acquired through a versatile acting career which started in 2000. Having appeared in numerous television and film productions, her net worth significantly increased. Since she is still active in the entertainment industry, her net worth continues to grow.

Katrina Law Net Worth $2 Million

Although born in Philadelphia, Law grew up in Deptford Township, New Jersey,of mixed descent as her mother is Taiwanese and her father has German and Italian ancestry; the two met during the Vietnam War. As a child she enrolled in a variety of activities such as dance, gym and karate classes, soccer, and voice coaching, which led to her being on the varsity track, cheerleading and soccer teams in her high school. During her teenage years, Katrina won the title of Miss New Jersey Teen USA and was a member of the National Honors Society.

She eventually graduated from The Richard Stockton College with a Theater degree, and decided to pursue a professional acting career. She continued to perfect acting in Philadelphia and New York, where she worked in theaters for several years. For her work as Olivia in “Twelfth Night” and the Bride in “Blood Wedding”, she received Irene Ryan A.C.T.F nominations. After some time, Katrina started appearing in television productions such as “CSI: Miami” (2002), “Chuck”(2009) and “Legend of the Seeker”(2010). She also portrayed lead roles in some independent films including “Lucky Numbers”(2000), “Bottomfeeders”(2001), “Emmett’s Mark”(2002) and “Alpha Males Experiment”(2009).

However, perhaps her most notable roles include those as Mira in the STARZ series “Spartacus: War of the Damned”(2010) and “Spartacus: Vengeance” (2012). Law also worked with director Adran Picardi and producers Don Le and Eric Ro in creating the web series “The Resistance”, in which she served as the official creator and director, and helped complete an action-oriented project named “Three minutes”(2011). Some of her other notable work includes roles in TV series “Third Watch” (2001), “Reba”(2002), “Ch:os:en”(2013), and “Guilt”(2015), but also movies such as “A New Tomorrow”(2007), “The Grind”(2010), “Death Valley”(2015) and “Checkmate”(2015).

When it comes to her more recent activity, Law has been recurrently appearing on CW’s TV show “Arrow”, in which she portrays the role of DC comic book character Nyssa Al Ghul.

Apart from her acting career, Law also likes music and is a bass player and singer of the Soundboard Fiction band.

Privately, Law has been married to actor Keith Andreen since 5th January 2013. Katrina supports several charities such as Children’s Miracle Network, The International Rescue Committee and Kitt Crusaders and also volunteers with Red Across America. She owns a black belt in Taekwondo, and is a big cat lover.
