The Bold and The Beautiful (B&B) spoilers reveal Bill Spencer (Don Diamont) once again proposes marriage to a skeptical Sheila Carter (Kimberlin Brown). What more can Bill do to convince this woman that he wants to marry her? Sheila is truly beginning to get on Bill’s last nerve.

The Bold And The Beautiful Updates – John Finnegan Is Determined To Protect His Family
