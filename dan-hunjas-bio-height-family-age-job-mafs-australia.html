MAFS Australia: Are Sandy Jawanda And Dan Hunjas Still Together?

However, this is not the case anymore. Dan has moved on from his beautiful Indian bride it seems.

Anyway, fans have been shocked to learn that Dan’s new bae, right now, is none other than a guest at his wedding.
