Justin Budfuloski Job

Justin Budfuloski is a Pain/ Scoliosis/Postural and Functional Online Specialist. He has over a decade of experience in the health and fitness industry formulating methodology and approach toward posture, movement mechanics, and health of the body.

The star started his journey after dealing with back pain, severe disk injuries, and dissatisfaction when he couldn’t figure out why these things were happening to himself and everyone else in the industry. So, after completing his studies at Cal State Northridge University, Justin joined 24 Hour Fitness as a personal trainer in September 2007.

After working there for over 8 years 8 months, Justin then started his career as a corrective exercise therapist at The Egoscue Method, and the next year, he went on to be a holistic specialist at Pain Academy.

Finally, with years of research and experience, he had created a new movement and exercises that enabled him to get out of pain. Thus, in June 2019, he started his own holistic health service called Symmetry Of Motion, with a motive to transform the world of scoliosis and posture.
