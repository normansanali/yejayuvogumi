Who is Lee Sang-Yi?

Lee Sang-Yi (이상이) from South Korea, is widely known as a popular actor, musical actor, and singer.

He rose to the limelight after featuring in the famous South Korean TV series Hometown Cha-Cha-Cha playing the role of JI Seong-Hung.

Apart from this, he is also known as the best member of the vocal group MSG Wannabe.

Lee Sang-Yi: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Famous South Korean actor, Lee Sang-Yi was born on November 27, 1991, in Ansan, Gyeonggi, South Korea. As of 2023, his age is 31.

He is of Korean nationality and belongs to the Caucasian ethnicity.

Also, Lee Sang-Yi follows Christianity religious. He has not talked about his parent’s real identity and siblings. Less known is known about his personal life.

Education

Moving to his educational background, Lee Sang-Yi attended Jeongji Elementary School and Wonil Middle School. After this, he moved to Anyang Arts High School to the Department of Theater and Films.

For his further education, he studied at the School of Drama, Department of Acting of Korea National University of Arts.

Lee Sang-Yi: Professional Life, Career

Lee Sang-Yi had a keen interest in acting since he was young. He mostly appeared in comedy films and Series.

His debut film was Taxi Driver playing the role of Ji Hoon in the year 2014. After featuring in this film he earned his name and fame in the entertainment industry.

In the same year, he played the role of the Police radio in the South Korean comedy film Sow Video.

In the year 2016, Lee Sang-Yi was featured in three films Call Me When You Eat as Repayment, Aend as Man, and Coward as Pumice Stone.

Likewise in 2019, he played the role of Huijung in the film, Battle of Noryang.
