Biography

Pat Casey was born in California on December 26, 1993. American BMX rider who won back-to-back X Games medals in 2012 and 2013. He is notable for being the first rider to successfully pull off the “decade backflip” and “double-decade backflip” in competition. He has cited Scotty Cranmer as one of his favorite BMX riders. He grew up in Southern California and began riding bikes when he was five.
