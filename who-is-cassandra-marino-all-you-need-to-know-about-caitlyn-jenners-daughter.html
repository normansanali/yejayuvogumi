Who Is Cassandra Marino? All You Need To Know About Caitlyn Jenner's Daughter

October 2024 · 3 minute read

Cassandra Marino is an American interior designer. She is well-known for being the daughter of TV personality and retired gold medallist in decathlon, Caitlyn Jenner previously known as Bruce Jenner. Despite being little discussed, Cassandra Marino is a prominent member of the well-known Kardashian-Jenner family and has a prominent place in the family tree. Unlike her other family members, Cassandra dislikes being in the spotlight. In contrast to her siblings, she never made an appearance in the 2007-debuting family reality series Keeping Up with the Kardashians.

Who Is Cassandra Marino?

Cassandra Marino was born on 10th June, 1980 in Los Angeles, California, United States. Cassandra Marino’s parents are Caitlyn Jenner and Chrystie Scott. While Caitlyn’s mother is an actress best known for TV shows and films like ESPN SportsCentury and SST: Death Flight, Caitlyn is a former Olympic gold medalist and television personality. Her parents divorced in 1981, a year after she was born. Her mother married Richard Scott following the divorce. In addition, her father married twice before undergoing a gender identity transformation.

The interior designer has one older biological brother and seven step-siblings from Caitlyn Jenner’s side. Burt Jenner is her biological brother. She is, however, Kim Kardashian’s step-sibling along with Rob, Kourtney, Kendall, Brody Jenner, and Kylie Jenner. But she’s the elusive one, not like any of her other siblings.

Cassandra Marino’s Career

Casey appears to be passionate about her work in interior design, as evidenced by her Instagram bio, which states that she enjoys building homes and bringing new ideas to life. Because of her seclusion, Casey is regarded as the most elusive Jenner.

At the moment, the family is based in Los Angeles, California, in the United States.
