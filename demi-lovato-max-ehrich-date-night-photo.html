Demi Lovato and Max Ehrich Share a Chic Date Night Photo

July 2024 · 2 minute read
  • Demi Lovato and fiancé Max Ehrich shared a photo to Instagram from their recent chic date night at L.A. hot spot Nobu.
  • Nobu is seemingly a favorite for the pair, as they celebrated their whirlwind engagement with close family and friends there last month.

Demi Lovato and Max Ehrich are still riding a post-engagement high.

The couple shared some PDA-filled photos and video to their Instagram Stories, including footage of the two heading out for date night at Nobu in Los Angeles over the weekend. In one photo shared to both of their accounts, the singer's husband-to-be planted a romantic kiss on her cheek, while she stared directly into the camera. Showing off a velvet, ruched dusty rose-hued minidress, Lovato had her hair pulled back and accessorized with Amina Muaddi stiletto heels and gold hoop earrings.

"Date night at Nobu! I love you @maxehrich - also shout out to @brianbonifassi for the pic!!! ❤️," wrote Lovato alongside the photo.

The "I Love Me" singer assured fans that she and her fiancé were dining out responsibly, however, taking to her Instagram Story to share a selfie of her and her beau donning face masks together before being sat at their table.

Demi Lovato //Instagram

Demi Lovato and Max Ehrich

Seemingly a preferred date night spot for the pair, Nobu is also where the couple threw an intimate dinner party following their Malibu beachside engagement last month, with Lovato getting emotional a few times over the course of the evening.

"Demi could not stop smiling. She said the engagement was a total surprise. You could tell it was both exciting and overwhelming for her. She even got teary-eyed a few times. It was all very emotional," a source told People at the time. "They kept kissing throughout the dinner. They are a very cute couple. Demi could not stop staring at her ring — it's huge and absolutely stunning."
