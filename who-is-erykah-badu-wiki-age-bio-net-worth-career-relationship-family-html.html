Who is Erykah Badu? Wiki, Age, Bio, Net Worth, Career, Relationship,Family

August 2024 · 3 minute read
  • Erykah Badu, whose real name is Erica Abi Wright, is a renowned American singer-songwriter, record producer, and actress.
  • She was born on 26th February 1971 in Dallas, Texas, United States, and is now 51 years old.
  • Erykah’s music style is influenced by R&B, soul, and hip-hop, and she gained fame with her debut album “Baduism” in 1997, earning her the title “Queen of Neo Soul” by music critics.

Erykah Badu’s Daughter

  • Erykah Badu has two daughters: Puma Sabti Curry and Mars Merkaba Thedford.
  • Puma Sabti Curry, her first daughter, was born on 5th July 2004, making her 18 years old.
  • Mars Merkaba Thedford, her second daughter, was born on 1st February 2009, and she is 13 years old.

Erykah Badu’s Husband and Kids

  • Erykah Badu is currently engaged to Jaron Adkison, as revealed through her Instagram picture.

  • She has three children: Seven Sirius Benjamin, Puma Sabti Curry, and Mars Merkaba Thedford.
