Kane Brown Bio (Wiki)

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Who is Kane Brown?

Kane Brown is an American country music singer and songwriter. Similarly, Kane Brown also released his first EP, titled Closer, in June 2015.

Likewise, his new single, “Used to Love You Sober”, was released in October 2015.

After that, he also signed with RCA Nashville in early 2016, the actual song was included on his EP Chapter 1, which was released in March 2016.

Besides that, he also released his first full-length album, the self-titled Kane Brown, on December 2, 2016.

Kane Brown: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Nationality

Kane was born in Northwest Georgia, Chattanooga, Tennessee, in the United States on October 21, 1993.

His mother’s name is Tabatha Brown but his father’s name is unknown.

His birth name is Kane Allen Brown. He has a sibling namely, Heidi Swafford.

Kane Brown belongs to American nationality and English ethnicity. His birth sign is Libra.

Talking about his education, firstly, he attended Red Bank High School. Then, he attended Lakeview-Fort Oglethorpe High School. After that, he attended Soddy-Daisy High School.

Kane Brown: Professional Career

Talking about his profession, he auditioned for both ‘American Idol’ and ‘X-Factor’ in 2013 and was selected for the latter.

Unfortunately, after quitting ‘X-Factor,’ he began posting his covers of popular singles online.

Whereas, his videos included covers of songs by the likes of Brantley Gilbert, Billy Currington, Alan Jackso, and Lee Brice.

Whereas, his cover of George Strait’s ‘Check Yes or No’ went viral and his online popularity reached great new heights.

Likewise, after becoming an online music sensation, Kane Brown also took the next step by releasing his first EP, titled ‘Closer,’ on June 2, 2015.

The other singles he released during this period were ‘Last Minute Late Night’, and ‘I Love That I Hate You.’ Therefore, he also released his EP ‘Chapter 1’.

Kane Brown launched his own label, 1021 Entertainment, in association with Sony Nashville on February 11, 2021.

He released the song “Leave You Alone” on March 3, 2022, after teasing it on social media in late 2021. On July 18, 2022, his third studio album, Different Man, was announced, and on September 9, 2022, the album was released.

Achievements and Awards

In talking about his lifetime achievements and awards, he won CMT Music Awards for Collaborative Video of the Year for “What Ifs”.

Likewise, Kane Brown won the American Music Awards for Favorite Country Male Artist for Himself.

Similarly, he won the American Music Award for Favorite Country Album for Kane Brown.

Then, Kane Brown won the American Music Award for Favorite Country Song for “Heaven”.

Brown won Video of the Year at the ACM Awards for “Worldwide Beautiful” in 2021, making him the first black person to do it.

He was named to the Time 100 in 2021, Time’s annual list of the world’s most influential individuals.
