Love After Lockup's Derek And Monique Have Just About The Most Turbulent Relationship Of Any Cast Me

September 2024 · 6 minute read

WeTV's 'Love After Lockup' follows couples whose romantic journeys are complicated by the fact that one partner is currently incarcerated while the other patiently waits for them outside.

The complicated yet fascinating connection between Monique Robinson and Derek Warner Jr. after their release from prison was a highlight of Season 5. Though many viewers wonder how Love After Lockup couples meet, with this duo, it was quite simple. One night while Monique was on Facebook, she stumbled into a group for inmates looking to become pen pals.

After finally meeting Derek in person after nearly two years, Monique was able to put her trust worries to rest, especially with insecurities surrounding her weight. Derek was all she had hoped for and more. But suddenly everything went downhill.

Monique Installed Cameras To Make Sure Derek Wasn’t Cheating On Her

Not long after Derek's release, they were being followed by a strange car. Although Derek claimed he didn't recognize the driver, he has the same suspicion as Monique that it was an ex-lover. With his freedom restored, Derek maybe hoped to reconnect with a former lover.

Given his fearless personality, it baffles Monique that he would ever bring a date back to his grandmother's place. Given the level of distrust between them, their prospects for a happy future together are dim.

Derek and Monique go to see his grandmother. While Monique is in Chicago, Derek will be sleeping alone in his room, so she looks for places to hide security cameras.

The ultimate goal was for her to return to Cleveland with all of her belongings and settle in with him permanently. It almost seemed like the Love After Lockup couple would beat the odds, but she has doubts that her partner will be responsible in her absence.

“I’d like to see if I can locate suitable places to put the cameras so I can keep an eye on him when I’m at home in Chicago,” Monique tells producers.

He had no notion that his relationship with her may be shakier than he thinks it is.

Derek Did Cheat On Monique, Confirming Her Suspicions

It turns out that Monique was right to be wary about Derek's commitment as a partner. Monique finally comes clean about catching Derek cheating on her after his release from jail.

Because of this, she broke up with him or made attempts to, but Derek is making every effort to get her back. There's no denying that Derek messed up, but he's making amends and trying his best to win Monique back.

“I was unfaithful to Monique because our relationship was in a gray area. I didn’t feel like it was a relationship. I was wrong for that. I shouldn’t be cheating. I shouldn’t be stepping out.” Derek informs the cameras during a confessional.

“Since Monique been back in Chicago, we’ve been talking every day, texting, laughing like we almost like we back to that good place where I was going to propose,” Derek mentioned to the producer.

Having spent some time in Chicago, Monique has returned to her home state of Ohio. She and her sisters are in the car discussing Derek's infidelity. She hasn't seen Derek in over a month, Monique tells her siblings, but she's eager to find out the truth about a lot of things.

Her siblings worry that she won't be able to put the past behind her and start over. Monique claims that Derek has expressed a desire to repair their relationship by requesting her return to Ohio. After learning that Derek had been cheating on her, Monique is thinking about trying to make amends with him.
