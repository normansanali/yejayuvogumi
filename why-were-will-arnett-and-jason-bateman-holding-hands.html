Fans of the iconic duo Jason Bateman and Will Arnett understand their incredible friendship and how it has stood the test of time. It's never a surprise to see the two together at events or working on a project. Additionally, the comedic duo is often seen in interviews or on their podcast, sharing jokes and funny stories. All of these have come to be expected by the fans, who love the camaraderie the two share.

However, in May 2013, photos began circulating that brought confusion to people everywhere. What was in the images that caused so many people to question the actor's intentions?

Paparazzi Caught Jason Bateman And Will Arnett Holding Hands

There are many photos of both Jason Bateman and Will Arnett where they are laughing or engaging in a joke. These are often paired with a multitude of interviews in which they poke fun at each other and reveal embarrassing stories. However, the photo series that filled people's screens in May 2013 deviated from the usual images that fans are used to seeing.

In a set of photographs, the paparazzi caught the stars walking down a sidewalk in New York City, holding hands. Within some of the images, you can see the actors staring into each other's eyes. Additional photos capture Jason Bateman pointing at an object off in the distance with Will following his gaze.

While it's unknown where the two were coming from, going to, or what they were doing, fans have their fair share of speculations. Some think they could be coming from their joint office, where they conduct business for their digital production company DumbDumb, or perhaps coming from or going to a recording session of their podcast SmartLess (with Sean Hayes).

Whatever the destination or purpose, the two looked to be enjoying themselves in the beautiful weather.

However, Will and Jason were caught on their leisurely stroll at the same time as when the Bluth Frozen Banana Stand appeared in Times Square. Were the two grabbing a frozen banana to commemorate the return of their hit show Arrested Development?

Some speculate that they were helping to run the operation. However, there didn't appear to be any celebrity sightings of them punching the clock and slinging banana treats.

Will Arnett Offers One Explanation

There are many things that fans know about Will Arnett due to his years in the public eye. However, the hand-holding stroll he shared with Jason Bateman in New York City is one thing they didn't know the story behind.

For his part, Will Arnett offers that Jason was taking the bit seriously and has some surprising and significant emotion behind the agreed-upon prank for the paparazzi.

Will Arnett starts the explanation by stating that the original idea was to put on a pretend public display of affection for the paparazzi the two saw down the sidewalk. While it was done as a joke, Will said that Jason took it quite seriously and didn't treat it as humorous but as an actual display of emotion.

Although Will continued with the act, he admits to being thrown off because it was obvious that Jason was taking it to another level that he wasn't expecting or comfortable with.

Jason Bateman Has His Own Version Of Events

While Jason Bateman takes some things, like his diet, very seriously, there are many things in which he goes the opposite direction. When it comes to joking around and pranking others, he is all in.

On Conan O'Brien, the host asked him about the series of images and pushed for an explanation. When it comes to his story behind the photo, Jason Bateman declared that the opposite of what Will offers was the truth.

The reasoning behind the hand-holding stroll, in Jason's words, is because he and Will Arnett are actually a couple and not just friends. While he, Jason, is okay with the public being aware of their relationship, Will is a bit more reserved in divulging the information.

Jason continues to say that even though Will is trying to play the situation off as a one-time prank, the two are actually taking a serious step towards cementing their status as a couple in the pictures. Referencing the image in which Jason is pointing at something in the distance, the actor says he is pointing out a new place for them to live together.

In other words, the two were embarking on a real estate search to start their new lives together. He ended the explanation with a greeting to Arnett, calling him "honey."

Which side of the story is the correct version of events? Although Jason and Will have different versions, one thing is for sure. The two actors and comedians know how to enjoy life and keep their fans laughing while enjoying their friendship at every given chance, which is a nice change from all the seriousness in the world.
